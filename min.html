Recipe: Pancakes


Cooking Time:20 min Origin: Greece

"RISE AND SHINE IT'S PANCAKES TIME."
We are sharing with you the recipe for this dish
here with you

Ingredients


  • Wheat flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Melted butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Vegetable oil

    • Here's the secret to the fluffiest pancakes.


      Step 1
      Combine the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
      Slowly pour in the milk and mix as well.

      Step 2
      Add the eggs, vanilla extract, and vegetable oil.
      Whisk the batter thoroughly until there are no more lumps.

      Step 3
      Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat.
      Coat with a non-stick cooking spray, butter or vegetable oil to prevent sticking.

      Step 4
      Spoon the batter onto the pan and cook each side until it is golden brown.
      Use a spatula to flip the pancakes and to remove them when they are finished.

      Step 5
      Serve immediately.
      Add maple syrup, fruit, whipped cream or powdered sugar if desired.
